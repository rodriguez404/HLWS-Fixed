EXAMPLES OF WRITING INTO CHANGELOG

If your changelog consists of 1-3 lines, you can write them like this:

- [change] - [name]

Examples:
- Spectre: Bone Servants: Cooldown 10s -> 11s - rodriguez
- lag optimisations - rodriguez


If your changelog is big of multiple lines, you can try documenting all your changes with one name above the list:

Example:
---rodriguez
- Life Glyph: complete rework to Magister Glyph (Lifesteal+Energy)
- Added hotkeys for all shops
- Phase Smith: Cannon Image: fixed target filters
- Assassin: Surging Strike: damage 75 -> 150


You are not forced to document "exactly" same as in given examples, but you should make your changelog clear and exhaustive, yet short enough.
Avoid logging like this:

Example 1 [TO AVOID]:
- Increased cooldowns for some ultimates - rodriguez [what ultimates? For what amount increased? Unclear logs will be denied.]

Example 2 [TO AVOID]:
- Reduced HP of Spectre's Bone Servants by 20% on all levels. It was made to negate his late-game potential, as he was getting unnecessarily good for how easy he is to play, considering being immortal hero. - rodriguez
[Noone, including me, will read that. That log would be disapproved. Only state actual changes and not your entire thought process. First sentence would be enough]
